ExtraSuite for Google Drive

ExtraSuite connects Claude Code, Codex, and Gemini CLI to your Google Workspace. Ask your AI to read, analyze, and edit spreadsheets directly.

Get Started

How it works

1

Install the skill

Run a one-time install command to add Google Sheets access to Claude Code, Codex, or Gemini CLI.

2

Share your document

Share your Google Sheet with your personal service account email. You control what the AI can access.

3

Ask your AI

Use natural language to ask Claude, Codex, or Gemini to read, analyze, or modify your spreadsheet.

What you can do

Prompt

"Read the sales data from my Q4 spreadsheet and create a summary with trends"

Result

Claude reads the data, identifies patterns, and writes a formatted summary with charts to a new sheet.

Prompt

"Clean up the contact list - remove duplicates, fix phone number formatting"

Result

Claude processes hundreds of rows, standardizes formats, and highlights changes for your review.

Prompt

"Generate a weekly report from this raw data and email it to the team"

Result

Claude transforms raw metrics into a polished report with insights and action items.

Prompt

"Compare these two budget spreadsheets and highlight the differences"

Result

Claude analyzes both sheets, creates a diff summary, and flags significant variances.
