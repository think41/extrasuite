Security

How ExtraSuite keeps your data safe

ExtraSuite agents can only access what you explicitly share with them.

How It Works

Each employee is assigned a dedicated virtual agent with its own email address. If you share a Google Doc or Google Sheet with your agent's email address, the agent can access only that document, and only with the permission level you choose (view, comment, or edit).

If you do not share a document with your agent, the agent cannot access it.

You Stay in Control

  • Revoke access instantly

    Remove the agent from the document's sharing settings in Google Drive.

  • Full transparency

    Any edits made by the agent appear clearly in version history, under the agent's identity.

  • Easy rollback

    Review what changed and undo the agent's edits at any time using Google's built-in version controls.

Technical Safeguards

Short-lived tokens

Access tokens expire after 1 hour and cannot be refreshed by the agent.

No stored credentials

No long-lived service account keys are used or distributed. Tokens are generated on-demand.

Scoped permissions

Tokens only grant access to Google Sheets, Docs, Slides, and read-only Drive access.

Domain restriction

Only employees with authorized email domains can use this service.

Security Guarantees

  • Agents cannot access documents unless you explicitly share them
  • Access is limited to the permission level you choose
  • All agent edits are attributable, auditable, and reversible
  • No long-lived credentials are exposed to agents
  • Tokens are never exposed in browser URLs or history
