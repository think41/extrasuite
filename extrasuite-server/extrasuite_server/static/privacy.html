Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

ExtraSuite is an authentication service that enables AI agents to access Google Workspace APIs on your behalf. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the authentication service:

  • Email address: Your Google account email address, used to identify your account.
  • Session data: A session cookie to maintain your login state.

What We Do NOT Collect or Store

  • OAuth refresh tokens: We do not store your Google OAuth refresh tokens.
  • Document contents: ExtraSuite does not read, store, or access your Google Docs, Sheets, or Drive files.
  • Personal files: We have no access to your personal data stored in Google Workspace.

ExtraSuite only facilitates token creation. The CLI tools or AI agents that use these tokens are responsible for their own data handling.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you via Google OAuth
  • Generate short-lived service account tokens (valid for 1 hour)
  • Maintain your session to avoid repeated authentication

Data Storage

  • Generated access tokens expire after 1 hour and are not stored server-side
  • Session cookies are used to maintain login state

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to third parties. Your data is used solely for operating this authentication service.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact your administrator.
