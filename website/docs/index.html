How It Works

Set up once, use everywhere

0

Install an AI Agent

Get Claude Code, Gemini CLI, or Codex CLI on your machine.

1

Connect Your AI

Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI assistants.

2

Authenticate Once

Sign in with Google. Your AI gets a secure, time-limited token.

3

Share Documents

Share specific files with your AI, just like sharing with a colleague.

4

Get Work Done

Describe what you need. Your AI reads, writes, and updates your docs.

Built with Security First

Enterprise-grade security for your Google Workspace data

You Stay in Control

AI agents can only access documents you explicitly share. Revoke access anytime.

Short-Lived Tokens

Access tokens expire after 1 hour. No long-lived credentials stored.

Full Audit Trail

All changes appear in Google's version history. Review and undo instantly.

Enterprise IAM

Built on Google Cloud IAM with domain restrictions and service account impersonation.
